HERBAL GARDEN

A Virtual Herbal Garden will bridge the gap of physical gardens that are not accessible to everyone by offering a digital platform
where users can explore, learn, and understand the significance of various medicinal plants from the comfort of their homes.
It is an engaging, informative, and user-friendly platform which includes Interactive 3D Models: Realistic 3D models of
medicinal plants that users can rotate, zoom, and explore from different angles.Comprehensive details about each plant,
including its botanical name, common names, habitat, medicinal uses, and methods of cultivation.
